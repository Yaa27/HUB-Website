What is HUB?

HUB is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering young people with business skills and entrepreneurial knowledge. Through practical and engagement activities, HUB supports young individuals in developing their entrepreneurial mindset and confidence. The organization focuses on enhancing both practical and theoretical soft skills such as analytical thinking, international networking, and leadership. By fostering these skills, HUB aims to instill in the youth the confidence that they can drive change and shape the future of the business world. HUB is aiming to become the door of the future to the business world for all motivated and inspired individuals.

Our Vision

We offer opportunities that are designed to get insight into a business through a different lens with practical business cases and inspire young generations to think in a critical and innovative way.

Our Goal

We aim to create a global community and excellent leaders who thrive in innovative entrepreneurship ideas.

THE COMPETITION!

HUB Global Business Competition is an international two-round business case competition for high school students interested in entrepreneurship and solving business cases by creating products/services. Participants will be asked to gather teams of 2 to 5 persons and register using a Google application form.

Competition Structure

FIRST ROUND:

Participants: 50-80 teams

Task: Create a solution for a presented business case in the form of a short video and a business plan (Assessment criteria can be sent if needed)

Timeframe: 7 days to prepare and submit the solution.

Evaluation: Judges will evaluate submissions based on specified criteria and select the top 15% of teams to advance to the next round.

SECOND ROUND:

Participants: Finalists from the first round.

Task: Create a solution for a new business case in the form of a short video and a business plan. (Assessment criteria can be sent if needed)

Timeframe: 5 days to prepare and submit the solution.

Evaluation: Judges will select two winning teams.

The Prize

Total Prize: $600

First Place: $400

Second Place: $200

The prize money can be used to develop the winning ideas or for personal expenses, as discussed with the sponsors.

Eligibility

Sign up with a team of 2 to 5 members

14 to 18 years old as of competition start date

Students strongly interested in entrepreneurship all around the world

Able to participate in English

Excited to tackle global issues
